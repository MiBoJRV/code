What Is a Romance Scam

Stay Aware

  • Romance scams are a type of financial fraud where criminals exploit individuals looking for love and companionship online.
  • Scammers create fake profiles on dating websites, social media platforms, or online forums to establish trust with their victims. They develop intimate relationships with victims, only to exploit their emotions and manipulate them into sending money or sharing sensitive financial information.

Types of Romance Scheme Scam

  • Fake Identity Scams

    Scammers create attractive and enticing personas, often using stolen photos and personal information. They forge emotional connections with victims and gain their trust.

    Our Solution:

    We employ advanced AI algorithms to conduct thorough background checks on online profiles, helping us identify suspicious or fake identities early on.

  • Emergency Scams

    Scammers feign emergencies or dire situations, compelling victims to send money urgently to help their supposed loved ones.

    Our Solution:

    Through user education, we raise awareness about emergency scams and teach clients to verify the authenticity of such requests before taking any action.

  • Long-Distance Relationship Scams

    Scammers establish long-distance relationships, claiming to be overseas and unable to meet in person. They later ask for financial assistance to facilitate a meeting.

    Our Solution:

    We encourage users to exercise caution when engaging in long-distance relationships online and provide guidance on setting boundaries and avoiding financial involvement too soon.

  • Catfishing Scams

    Scammers deceive victims by pretending to be someone they are not, leading to emotional attachment and, ultimately, financial requests.

    Our Solution:

    Our team of expert fraud analysts scrutinizes online communications for inconsistencies and suspicious behavior, aiding in the early detection of catfishing attempts.

  • Military Romance Scams

    Scammers impersonate military personnel, taking advantage of their perceived trustworthiness to manipulate victims into sending money or personal information.

    Our Solution:

    We collaborate with military support organizations to verify the authenticity of military-related requests, ensuring our clients don’t fall victim to such scams.

  • Inheritance Scams

    Scammers claim to be beneficiaries of a large inheritance or lottery winnings, seeking financial assistance to release the funds.

    Our Solution:

    Our AI algorithms detect and flag messages related to inheritance or lottery claims, guiding clients to validate the legitimacy of such claims before taking any action.

  • Online Dating Site Subscription Scams

    Scammers lure victims into signing up for fake dating websites, tricking them into paying membership fees without any genuine dating prospects.

    Our Solution:

    We provide a list of reputable dating platforms and tips for verifying the legitimacy of dating websites and safeguarding clients from subscription scams.
